 **Bonus Question**:

 ```{=tex}
\begin{equation*}
\begin{split}
y & = \sin{\sqrt{2x}} \\
\dv{y}{x} & = \dv{x} \sin{\sqrt{2x}} \\
          & = \cos{\sqrt{2x}} \cdot \dv{x} \sqrt{2x} \\
          & = \cos{\sqrt{2x}} \cdot \frac{1}{2} 2x ^{-\frac 1 2} \cdot \dv{x} 2x \\
          & = \cos{\sqrt{2x}} \cdot \frac{1}{2} 2x ^{-\frac 1 2} \cdot 2 \\
          & = \cos{\sqrt{2x}} \cdot \frac{1}{2\sqrt{2x}} \cdot 2 \\
          & = \frac{\cos{\sqrt{2x}}}{\sqrt{2x}}
\end{split}
\end{equation*}
 ```
